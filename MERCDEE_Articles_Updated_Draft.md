# MERCDEE Articles of Incorporation — Updated Draft (Corporate Structure)

> Draft prepared as a text-based update to support end-to-end corporate structure revisions while keeping entity files separate.

## Article I — Name

The name of the corporation is **MERCDEE, Inc.** (the “Corporation”).

## Article II — Duration

The Corporation shall exist perpetually unless dissolved in accordance with law.

## Article III — Purpose

The Corporation is organized for any lawful business purpose, including operations, strategic partnerships, investment activities, advocacy initiatives, and related services as permitted by applicable law.

## Article IV — Principal Office and Registered Agent

1. **Principal Office:** The principal office shall be located at such place as designated by the Board of Directors.
2. **Registered Agent/Office:** The Corporation shall maintain a registered office and registered agent in the state of incorporation as required by law.

## Article V — Corporate Structure and Governance

1. **Board Authority:** Corporate powers shall be exercised by or under the authority of the Board of Directors.
2. **Officers:** Officers shall be appointed by the Board and may include President/CEO, Secretary, Treasurer/CFO, and additional officers as needed.
3. **Entity Structuring Powers:** The Corporation may form or acquire subsidiaries, establish affiliates, and enter holding, operational, and shared-services arrangements as approved by the Board.
4. **Corporate Group Oversight:** The Board may establish corporate governance controls, reporting lines, approval matrices, and risk controls across all controlled entities.
5. **Delegation and Committees:** The Board may delegate powers to officers and committees to the extent allowed by law and governing documents.

## Article VI — Capitalization

1. The Corporation is authorized to issue **10,000,000** shares of Common Stock (or such amount as specified in final state filing requirements).
2. The Board is authorized to issue shares for lawful consideration and to approve stock-related actions, subject to law.
3. Additional classes/series of shares may be authorized by amendment as permitted by law.

## Article VII — Director Liability Limitation

To the maximum extent permitted by law, directors shall not be personally liable to the Corporation or shareholders for monetary damages arising from breach of fiduciary duty, except where such limitation is not allowed by law.

## Article VIII — Indemnification

The Corporation shall indemnify directors, officers, employees, and agents to the fullest extent permitted by law, including advancement of expenses where appropriate.

## Article IX — Bylaws

Bylaws may be adopted, amended, or repealed by the Board unless otherwise provided by law or reserved to shareholders.

## Article X — Amendments

The Corporation reserves the right to amend, alter, or repeal any provision in these Articles in accordance with applicable law.

## Article XI — Incorporator

The incorporator name and address shall be stated in formation records and maintained in corporate documents.

# LBWG Articles of Incorporation — Updated Draft (Corporate Structure)

> Draft prepared as a text-based update to support end-to-end corporate structure revisions while keeping entity files separate.

## Article I — Name

The name of the corporation is **LBWG, Inc.** (the “Corporation”).

## Article II — Duration

The Corporation shall have perpetual existence unless dissolved according to applicable law.

## Article III — Purpose

The Corporation is organized for any lawful purpose for which corporations may be organized under applicable corporate law, including but not limited to advocacy, consulting, education, and related strategic initiatives.

## Article IV — Principal Office and Registered Agent

1. **Principal Office:** The principal business office shall be established at such place(s) as determined by the Board of Directors.
2. **Registered Agent and Office:** The Corporation shall continuously maintain a registered agent and registered office in the state of incorporation, as required by law.

## Article V — Corporate Structure and Authority

1. **Governing Body:** The business and affairs of the Corporation shall be managed under the direction of its Board of Directors.
2. **Officers:** The Corporation shall have officers as determined by the Board, which may include a Chief Executive Officer/President, Secretary, Treasurer/Chief Financial Officer, and such other officers as needed.
3. **Parent/Subsidiary/Affiliate Authority:** The Corporation may create, acquire, or hold interests in subsidiaries and affiliates, enter into management or services agreements, and restructure entities as approved by the Board and consistent with law.
4. **Intercompany Governance:** The Board may adopt intercompany governance standards, approval thresholds, and reporting requirements for controlled entities.
5. **Delegation:** The Board may delegate authority to officers, committees, and authorized agents, except where prohibited by law or governing documents.

## Article VI — Authorized Shares

1. The total number of shares the Corporation is authorized to issue is **10,000,000** shares of Common Stock, par value as set by Board resolution unless otherwise required by law.
2. The Board is authorized to issue shares from time to time for such consideration as it deems appropriate and lawful.
3. If preferred shares are later authorized, rights and preferences shall be set forth in an amendment or board-authorized designation filing as required by law.

## Article VII — Limitation of Director Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, a director of the Corporation shall not be personally liable to the Corporation or its shareholders for monetary damages for breach of fiduciary duty as a director, except as otherwise provided by law.

## Article VIII — Indemnification

The Corporation shall indemnify and advance expenses to directors, officers, employees, and agents to the fullest extent permitted by law, subject to procedures set by the Board and applicable statutes.

## Article IX — Bylaws

The Board of Directors is authorized to adopt, amend, or repeal the Bylaws unless otherwise reserved to shareholders by law.

## Article X — Amendments

The Corporation reserves the right to amend these Articles of Incorporation in any manner permitted by law.

## Article XI — Incorporator

The name and address of the Incorporator shall be stated in the formation filing and/or organizational records.

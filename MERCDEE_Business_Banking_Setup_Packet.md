# MERCDEE Business Banking Setup Packet

> Entity-specific, bank-ready onboarding checklist and information packet for **MERCDEE**.

## 1) Banking Objectives (MERCDEE)

- Open primary **Business Checking** account for operations.
- Open **Business Savings/Reserve** account for tax and strategic reserves.
- Configure digital treasury rails (ACH, wires, approvals, alerts).
- Assign authorized signers consistent with MERCDEE governance.
- Implement accounting integration and reconciliation controls.

### Accounts

- [ ] **MERCDEE Operating Checking**

### Services

- [ ] Online banking enrollment completed
- [ ] ACH + wire entitlements configured
- [ ] Dual approvals enabled for high-risk transactions
